Our Story

Roasted with Intention

It started with a simple belief: coffee should be more than a morning ritual. It should be an experience — from the farmers who grow it, to the hands that roast it, to the moment it touches your lips. At Ember & Bloom, every batch is roasted in small lots right here in our café, letting you taste the difference that care and craft make.
