Our Story

From a single roaster to a community gathering place.

Who We Are

Born from a Love of Coffee and Community

Ember & Bloom began in 2019 in a small corner shop with a single roaster, a handful of green beans, and an obsession with getting every cup just right. We believed that specialty coffee didn't have to be intimidating — it just had to be honest.

Six years later, we've grown into a full café and roastery, but the philosophy hasn't changed. We still roast in small batches. We still know our farmers by name. And we still believe that the best coffee is the one shared with good company.

Every bean we source is traceable to a specific farm or cooperative. We pay well above fair trade prices because we believe the people who grow our coffee deserve more than a minimum — they deserve recognition.

What Drives Us

Our Values

🌱

Ethical Sourcing

Direct trade relationships with farmers across 12 countries. We pay premiums that fund education, infrastructure, and sustainable farming practices at origin.

🔥

Small-Batch Roasting

Every roast is monitored by hand. We roast in lots of 15kg or less, adjusting temperature curves bean by bean to unlock each origin's unique character.

🤝

Community First

Our café is a gathering place — for morning regulars, afternoon studiers, and evening conversationalists. We host tastings, workshops, and open mic nights monthly.

♻️

Sustainability

Compostable cups, reusable tin program, zero-waste pastry kitchen. Our spent grounds go to local community gardens. Small steps, real impact.

✨

Craft Over Speed

We don't do fast coffee. Every drink is made to order, with attention to grind size, water temperature, and extraction time. Quality takes a moment — and it's worth it.

📚

Education

We offer free cuppings every Saturday morning and barista workshops monthly. We want you to understand and love your coffee as much as we do.
